Special projects

  • Annual Illinois School Report Cards: The only comprehensive web app by a state news site that reports the scores, demographics and financials from the state.
  • Poverty-Acheivement Index: Our award-winning look at the effect of poverty on school performance.
  • Simple map maker for online only: Experimental page that allows you to create a simple map, suitable for online use only.
  • Surplus military equipment in the suburbs: A seachable database of the military-grade items state and local police agencies have received from the Federal government.
  • The Story of Capt. Duckworth and The Soldiers Who Saved Her Life: Multimedia timeline for the anniversary of the crash that changed Duckworth's life.
  • Route 53 extension: Comprehensive look at what happens when a planned expressway/tollway is delayed for so long that suburbs grow up around the planning area. We used this tool to track where photographers were along the route and plotted them and their photos along the path.
